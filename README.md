# 🌐 ConnectSphere

**ConnectSphere** is an anonymous social media platform where users can freely share photos, videos, and messages without revealing their identity. It focuses on authentic interaction, privacy, and content-driven engagement—letting users connect without the pressure of personal identity.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 🔒 **Anonymous Experience** – No personal identity required
* 📸 **Media Sharing** – Upload and share photos & videos
* ❤️ **Engagement System** – Like posts and gain followers
* 💬 **Private Chat** – One-on-one anonymous conversations
* 🌍 **Explore Feed** – Discover trending and new content

---

## 🎯 Key Highlights

* Privacy-first design
* Real, unbiased interactions
* Simple and intuitive UI
* Built for open expression
